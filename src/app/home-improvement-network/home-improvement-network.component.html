FAQs

  • What is the InvestFar Home Improvement Network?

    InvestFar Home Improvement Network is a benefit designed to help direct Investor Club members to pre-screened contractors for home improvements. Network contractors have undergone an extensive and thorough background check for licensing, insurance, and financial stability. When requesting a network contractor referral, the member will be assigned one contractor who has satisfied the pre-screening credentialing process and is willing to back their work with a three-year workmanship warranty.

  • Who manages the contractors that are part of the Home Improvement Network?

    Crawford Contractor Connection® manages this network of over 4,500 independent contractors.

  • How long does it take for a network contractor to contact a member for general service requests?

    A network contractor referral request for a home improvement project is a non-emergency request. Under most conditions, the member will be in contact with a contractor within 48 hours.

  • How long does it take for a network contractor to contact the member for urgent requests?

    A call for assistance with home emergency services is a priority call and, under most conditions, the member will be contacted by a service provider within one hour.

  • How does the Home Improvement Network help you get a fair and reasonable estimate?

    Network contractors are required to use the same estimating system that is commonly used in insurance restoration work and Contractor Connection's Quality Assurance team reviews all proposals, based on industry standards, to help contractors provide you with a fair and reasonable estimate. You are then able to agree or further discuss your project estimate direct with the contractor.

  • What is the benefit of selecting a network contractor to the Investor Club members?

    Contractor Connection has completed the extensive, time consuming, and critical contractor screening process for the member. Network contractors must possess required state licensing, meet established program insurance coverage, maintain professional facilities and equipment, and perform background checks on their employees who will be on the property site. Participating network contractors may use subcontractors in some instances when handling general contracting assignments. Please note that neither Contractor Connection nor InvestFar, incscreens or performs background checks on subcontractors.

  • How does InvestFar know that network contractors are performing to program standards?

    Network contractors are managed for work quality and timeliness by Contractor Connection and, after an InvestFar Home Improvement Network referral project is completed, every member is provided a customer satisfaction survey to provide feedback on their experience with the contractor and quality of the contractor workmanship.
