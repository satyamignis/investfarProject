Find & Evaluate

Search our marketplace for off market properties from preforeclosures, for sale by owner, banked owned and nationwide wholesalers. In addition, search through MLS listed homes that include traditional, short sales, and foreclosure listings.

Once you’ve located a property and market of interest use our resources such as valuation reports, investor consultants or network peer to peer with IF Connect so you can thoroughly evaluate and make an informed decision.

Renovate

Property renovating can be a daunting task especially when you’re unfamiliar with a market but we have you covered. Search our database of nationwide vetted contractors, plumbers or financiers for renovations.

Post renovation list your property for FREE on InvestFar to sell or rent. If you’re interested in having the rehab done for you along with having a tenant placed, please reach out to our client support team directly for tailored solutions.

Acquire

Acquiring properties traditionally or by way of investments has been an antiquated process in terms of processing, due diligence and market knowledge. Automate acquisitions by using InvestFar tools and a secure payment system.

Our vetted international payments system lets you buy outright or bid on properties with confidence, buyers and sellers are verified automatically with a multistep process. After an agreement is made, send or receive legal documents with our tools and release funds for remote closings.

Rent & Manage

It can be difficult for residential estates and complexes to maintain a high level of communication between residents and management. With InvestFar management can now be taken into your own hands and automated for ease of communication.

Our property management tool allows contact to management as a resident, as well as allowing management to immediately respond to issues in real time. Simplify reporting capabilities and tenant approvals remotely.
